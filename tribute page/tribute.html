Malala Yousafzai

Malala Yousafzai is a Pakistani education activist and the youngest Nobel Prize laureate. Born in 1997, Malala defied the Taliban in Pakistan and demanded that girls be allowed to receive an education. In 2012, she survived an assassination attempt by a Taliban gunman and continued her advocacy with even more determination.
